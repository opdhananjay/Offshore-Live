OUR INDUSTRY

Drilling

Drilling in offshore locations refers to the process of extracting oil or natural gas from beneath the seabed. It involves drilling wells through the ocean floor to access hydrocarbon reservoirs beneath the seabed. Offshore drilling is a complex operation that requires specialized equipment, technology, and expertise due to the challenging environment of the ocean.

Construction

Offshore construction involves the building of structures and installations in marine environments away from the shore. It encompasses a wide range of activities and projects essential for offshore oil and gas exploration, renewable energy generation, marine transportation, and coastal protection.
